## Overview
This lesson explores Virtual and Augmented Reality integration for enhanced human-robot interaction in Physical AI systems, focusing on immersive interfaces and teleoperation.

## Learning Objectives
After completing this lesson, you will be able to:
- Implement VR/AR interfaces for robot control and monitoring
- Design immersive human-robot interaction experiences
- Evaluate the impact of immersive interfaces on robot operation

## VR/AR in Robotics
Virtual and Augmented Reality technologies provide powerful interfaces for controlling and monitoring Physical AI systems in more intuitive and immersive ways.

## Implementation Considerations
Consider the technical requirements and user experience factors when implementing VR/AR interfaces for robotics.

## Ethical and Social Implications
VR/AR interfaces can enhance human understanding of robot capabilities and limitations, improving safety and trust in human-robot interaction.
